AN INTERACTIVE AI ETHICS SIMULATION

What is this?

You just got hired as an AI Ethics Consultant at Nexus Corp, one of the biggest tech companies in the world. The company is about to launch ARIA -- an AI system that will control city power grids and decide who gets hired at major companies.

You have to make 5 big decisions about how ARIA gets built and used. Every choice you make will affect real people, the environment, and society. There are no easy answers here.

Warning: This simulation is based on real AI ethics issues. The harms here are not about killer robots. They are about energy, jobs, bias, and who actually has power over these systems.

Guattari: Three Ecologies Edwards: Digital Harm Braidotti: Posthumanism

How to play

Read each scenario carefully. Click the choice that you think is the right call. Watch your four meters -- Environment, Social Trust, Corporate Power, and Worker Wellbeing -- as they go up or down based on what you pick.

Every path leads to one of 4 possible endings. Your choices reveal what kind of ethics consultant you actually are.
